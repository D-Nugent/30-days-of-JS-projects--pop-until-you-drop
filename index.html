Rules of the Game

The goal of the game is to pop all of the balloons that appear in the window in the time allotted. You pop a balloon by clicking on it, the caveat is that to pop certain balloons you must be holding down the corresponding modifier key, pop a balloon with the wrong key and you'll get a penalty!
